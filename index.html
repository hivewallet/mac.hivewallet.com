Hive Mac

A safe home for your bitcoins, right on your Mac.

Download

Version 1.4.1, requires OSX 10.7 or newer.

Send bitcoins to your friends easily

If you send or receive bitcoins from someone often, you can add them to the contact list for easier access. One contact can have any number of Bitcoin addresses assigned.

Make secure payments to merchants

Hive Mac supports a method of Bitcoin payments called Payment Protocol (BIP70), which is used by payment processors like Coinbase and BitPay and most merchants who accept Bitcoin.

Use Hive in your own language

Thanks to volunteer translators from the Bitcoin community, Hive Mac has been translated to over 30 languages so far. If your language is not on the list yet, you can contribute a new translation.

Keep backup copies of your wallet

Hive can make backups of your wallet automatically to your Dropbox or Google Drive folder, and can also monitor if Time Machine backups are being done regularly.

Get notified when you receive money

Hive shows you notifications when you receive a transaction or when your transaction is confirmed.

Why Use Hive?

Simple

Hive is designed to be easy to start with and easy to use.

Secure

The keys to your bitcoins are accessible only to you and encrypted with your password.

Private

You don't need to give anyone your name or address, and no one can stop you from making any transactions you want.

Native

Hive is built specifically for the Mac, using Apple's tools and application frameworks.
